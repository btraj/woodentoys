Frequently Asked Questions

What age is this toy suitable for?

Our wooden building blocks are designed for children aged 2-4 years. However, they can be enjoyed by older children as well. Due to small parts, they are not suitable for children under 2 years of age.

What wood are the blocks made from?

Our blocks are crafted from sustainably sourced neem wood, which is known for its durability and natural antibacterial properties. The wood is carefully selected and processed to ensure smooth edges and splinter-free surfaces.

How do I clean the blocks?

To clean the blocks, simply wipe them with a damp cloth. Do not submerge in water or use harsh cleaning chemicals, as this may damage the wood or finish. Allow to air dry completely before storing.

Do you offer replacement pieces?

Yes, we understand that pieces can sometimes get lost. Individual replacement blocks can be purchased by contacting our customer service team. Please note that replacement pieces are subject to availability.
