Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our wooden toys, shipping, returns, and more

Shipping Information

We process all orders within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping within India takes 3-5 business days after processing. Express shipping (₹150 extra) takes 1-2 business days. International shipping is available to select countries and typically takes 7-14 business days.

Yes, we ship to select countries internationally. International shipping rates vary by destination and order weight. Please note that international orders may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over ₹1,500 within India. International orders and orders below ₹1,500 will have shipping calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly through the courier's website. If you haven't received a tracking number within 3 business days of placing your order, please contact our customer service team.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in their original packaging. Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective. To initiate a return, please email us at returns@woodentoys.com with your order number and reason for return.

To return a product, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@woodentoys.com with your order number and reason for return
  2. We'll send you a return authorization and shipping instructions
  3. Package the item securely in its original packaging if possible
  4. Ship the item back using the provided return label or your preferred carrier
  5. Once we receive and inspect the return, we'll process your refund

Once we receive your returned item, we'll inspect it and process your refund within 3-5 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Please note that it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or credit card company.

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Please include photos of the damaged item and packaging. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you. In some cases, we may ask you to return the damaged item using a prepaid shipping label we provide.

Toy Safety

Yes, all our wooden toys are designed with safety as our top priority. They are made from sustainably sourced, natural wood and finished with non-toxic, child-safe paints and oils. All our products comply with international safety standards including IS 9873 (Indian Standard for Safety of Toys) and EN 71 (European Safety Standard for Toys).

We use only non-toxic, child-safe finishes on our wooden toys. Depending on the product, we use one or more of the following:

  • Food-grade mineral oil
  • Beeswax
  • Natural plant-based oils (such as linseed or jojoba)
  • Water-based, non-toxic paints that are free from lead, phthalates, and other harmful chemicals

All finishes are thoroughly tested to ensure they are safe for children, including those who may put toys in their mouths.

Some of our toys designed for older children may contain small parts that could pose a choking hazard for children under 3 years. We clearly label all our products with age recommendations and warnings about small parts where applicable. Each product description includes detailed safety information, and we encourage parents to supervise young children during play.

To clean our wooden toys, we recommend:

  • Wiping with a damp cloth using mild soap if necessary
  • Avoiding soaking in water or using harsh cleaning chemicals
  • Allowing toys to air dry completely before storing
  • Periodically applying a small amount of food-grade mineral oil or beeswax to maintain the wood's natural beauty (for unfinished or oil-finished toys)

With proper care, our wooden toys can last for generations and be passed down as heirlooms.

Age Suitability

Each of our products includes a recommended age range based on developmental appropriateness and safety considerations. You can filter products by age group on our shop page. Additionally, product descriptions include information about the skills each toy helps develop, which can help you choose toys that match your child's current developmental stage.

For babies under 12 months, we recommend:

  • Wooden rattles with smooth, rounded edges
  • Teething rings made from untreated hardwood
  • Simple grasping toys that are easy for small hands to hold
  • Wooden baby gyms with hanging wooden elements

All our toys for babies are made with rounded edges, have no small parts, and use extra-safe finishes that are suitable for mouthing.

For toddlers aged 1-3 years, we recommend:

  • Push and pull toys that encourage walking and movement
  • Simple stacking toys and building blocks
  • Shape sorters and simple puzzles
  • Nesting toys and containers for filling and emptying
  • Simple role-play toys like wooden phones or kitchen items

These toys support the rapid development of fine and gross motor skills during the toddler years while being durable enough to withstand enthusiastic play.

Yes, we offer many toys suitable for children aged 4-6 years and older, including:

  • More complex building sets with various shapes and sizes
  • Wooden puzzles with increasing levels of difficulty
  • Board games and strategy games
  • Detailed role-play toys like dollhouses, kitchens, and tool benches
  • Arts and crafts supplies made from natural materials
  • Musical instruments designed for children

These toys encourage problem-solving, creativity, and social skills development as children grow.

General Questions

All our wooden toys are handcrafted in India by skilled artisans. We work with small workshops and family businesses that specialize in traditional woodworking techniques. By producing locally, we support local communities, reduce our carbon footprint, and ensure close oversight of quality and ethical manufacturing practices.

We use a variety of sustainably sourced hardwoods, including:

  • Neem wood - naturally antibacterial and resistant to insects
  • Sheesham (Indian Rosewood) - durable with beautiful grain patterns
  • Rubberwood - sustainable wood from rubber plantations
  • Mango wood - reclaimed from trees that no longer produce fruit

We never use endangered wood species, and we prioritize wood from managed forests or reclaimed sources.

Yes, we offer eco-friendly gift wrapping for ₹75 per item. Our gift wrapping uses recycled kraft paper, natural jute twine, and a handwritten gift message on a seed paper tag that can be planted to grow wildflowers. You can select gift wrapping during checkout and add your personalized message.

Yes, we offer customization and personalization services for many of our wooden toys. Options include:

  • Name engraving on select toys
  • Custom color choices for painted elements
  • Personalized message engraving for special occasions
  • Custom toy designs for bulk orders (minimum quantities apply)

Please contact us at custom@woodentoys.com with your requirements, and we'll be happy to discuss options and pricing. Custom orders typically require 2-3 weeks for production.
